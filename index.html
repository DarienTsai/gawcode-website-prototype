WE ARE CODE!

The only computer science club on campus

Who Are We?

We are the only computer science club on Glen A. Wilson’s campus. We’re a group of people who think that everyone should try out programming, not just for the sake of finding a career, but for fun and appreciation.

What We Do.

We strive to create a community of coders. Our activities consist of weekly coding workshops, where members can learn and work on cool projects!
